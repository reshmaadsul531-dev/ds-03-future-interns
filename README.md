# Marketing Funnel Analysis – Data Analytics Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a marketing funnel dataset to understand how users move from **leads to customers**. The goal is to identify conversion rates, drop-off points, and factors that influence successful conversions.

Marketing funnel analysis helps businesses improve their **sales strategy, campaign targeting, and customer engagement**, ultimately increasing revenue.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze **lead to customer conversion rates**
* Identify **drop-off points in the marketing funnel**
* Compare performance across **different contact channels**
* Generate **data-driven recommendations** to improve conversion

---

## 📊 Dataset

Dataset used: **Bank Marketing Dataset**

The dataset contains marketing campaign data including customer demographics, contact methods, and campaign results.

Key Columns:

* `age` – Age of customer
* `job` – Job type
* `balance` – Bank balance
* `contact` – Communication type
* `campaign` – Number of contacts performed
* `deposit` – Whether the customer subscribed to the term deposit (Yes/No)

## 📈 Key Metrics

* **Total Leads:** 11,162
* **Total Customers:** 5,289
* **Conversion Rate:** 47.38%
* **Drop-off Rate:** 52.62%

---

## 📊 Visualizations

The project includes visualizations such as:

* Overall conversion distribution
* Conversion by contact channel
* Conversion by age group

These visualizations help identify patterns and opportunities for improving marketing performance.

---

## 🔎 Key Insights

* Nearly **half of the leads converted into customers**, indicating moderate campaign success.
* A significant portion of leads **drop off before conversion**, suggesting room for optimization.
* Certain contact channels show **better conversion performance** compared to others.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on **high-performing contact channels**
* Improve targeting strategies for **low-converting segments**
* Optimize follow-up strategies to reduce lead drop-off
* Use data-driven marketing campaigns to increase conversion rates
